Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order? +
You can track your order using the tracking link sent to your email or by logging into your account and visiting 'My Orders' section. Enter your order ID to get real-time updates.
What is your return policy? +
We offer 30-day hassle-free returns. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Return shipping is free for Prime members.
How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary by location.
How do I change or cancel my order? +
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, please contact support immediately for assistance.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, NetBanking, EMI options, and digital wallets like PayPal, Google Pay.
How do I reset my password? +
Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page. You'll receive an email with reset instructions. Follow the link to create a new password.
